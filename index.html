NAMKEEN - NUST LaTeX Templates

NUST LaTeX Templates for Presentations and Posters

Namkeen is a unique way of cooking simple, hygienic, tasty, and healthy food.

The same is being adopted in this theme, which is currently working being developed by Associate Professor, Dr. Hasan Ali Khattak at the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad. This theme was conceived out of need, as during an international conference it was felt that there is no elegant theme that matches the needs and styles as defined under the NUST branding. The theme has also been posted as Overleaf Theme under Creative Commons CC BY 4.0.

OPTIONS

--namkeen-poster

Namkeen Poster uses Latex to Generate an A0 size poster and the rest of the code is self explanatory. Download Here

--namkeen-sidebar

Namkeen sidebar theme is really interesting and cool, it helps you to use an elegant design with the help of latex. Download Here

--namkeen-powerpoint

These are powerpoint files both in Office 2007 as well as Office 2013+ (Office365) format, just double click and begin writing. Download Here

--namkeen-simple

This uses beamer and enhances is to make it as near to PowerPoint theme as possible. Download Here

--namkeen-beamer

Finally this is the namkeen beamer theme, it just changes the beamer colors and uses NUST colors in RGB(0,72,121) Download Here

--other

Feel free to download and work through the theme and template files, in case you have some improvements please comment and pull requests as this will surely improve the theme overall. Hope this will be a great benefit to all NUSTians.
